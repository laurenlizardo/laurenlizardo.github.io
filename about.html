Lauren Lizardo

GAME DEVELOPER


Hi there! 👋🏽I'm a game developer based in the Pacific Northwest. I like to make things, from food to PC builds.

I have four years under my belt creating games and experiences for XR platforms. Since the beginning of my career, I have collaborated with startups and small studios on a variety of XR projects, from arcade games to quality assurance apps. Those works were created for platforms such as iOS, Android, HoloLens, and Oculus Quest.

Although my professional career is based on developing for XR platforms, delivering memorable experiences through video games is my ultimate passion. I specialize in programming and have a deep interest in systems design.

Before the start of my career, I attended the Academy of Art University and earned my BFA in Game Development.
